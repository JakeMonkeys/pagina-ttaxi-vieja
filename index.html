Can you pay for the taxi service with a card?

Yes, we accept credit and debit card payments to give you more convenient and secure payment options.

Can I book a taxi for a specific day and time?

Yes, we offer advance booking services for specific days and times. Please contact us in advance to schedule your trip.

Do you offer transportation services to the airport?

Yes, we provide airport transportation services. Please contact us to schedule your transfer in advance.

Can I request a receipt or invoice for my trip?

Yes, you can request a receipt or invoice for your trip. Our drivers are equipped to provide you with proof at the end of the trip.

Do you offer transportation service out of town or to distant destinations?

We offer transportation services outside the city or to distant destinations, according to availability and prior coordination. We recommend contacting us in advance to discuss your specific needs and schedule your trip.

What should I do if I forget something in the taxi after my trip?

If you forget something in the taxi after your trip, please contact us as soon as possible to inform us of the missing item. We will do our best to recover it and return it to you as soon as possible. We recommend that you provide specific details about the item and the trip in question to facilitate the search
